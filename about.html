Our Story

Meet Captain Osman

Born & Raised in Oak Ridge

Roatan On The Fish is built on a lifetime of maritime heritage. Captain **Osman Alvarado** has spent his life navigating the intricate mangrove channels and deep blue horizons of the East End.

Based out of the historic fishing village of Oak Ridge, we avoid the crowded tourist traps to give you an authentic island experience. From expert deep-sea fishing to the serene beauty of Port Royal, you're experiencing the island through the eyes of a local.

"We don't just show you the island; we show you our home."

— Capt. Osman Alvarado
